Athletics

Welcome to Our Athletics Page

A lot of the community around campus is built through our athletic programs and competitions. Here at SMWC we have 16 varsity teams competing in various sports throughout the year. These teams represent our school with pride and dedication. Not only do they compete at a high level, but they also serve as role models for the broader student body. Within their teams and outside of their teams they are able to build community. As athletes at SMWC, they exemplify the values of hard work, teamwork, and perseverance, representing who we are as a school in whole.

One of the best aspects of our athletic program is game day atmosphere and the sense of school spirit that it creates. Teams here at SMWC support one another and show pride in their school. Even if you are not an athlete, you can still participate in the school spirit and support your friends by attending the games.

Esports

The SMWC eSports and Gaming Club is a vibrant and welcoming community of casual and competitive video gamers. The club prides itself on being a competitive, yet, relaxed environment for gaming enthusiasts of all levels. An eSports participant can hone on their independent competitive skills in preparation for tournaments within the club and against other collegiate teams.

Cheer

The SMWC Cheer Team is a coed team that provides college students with the ability to continue improving their skills in cheerleading. The team will provide school spirit at athletic events and other school-related functions. In the future, the team will begin competitions representing SMWC at events against other intercollegiate institutions. The club is active during the fall and spring semesters.

Cycling

SMWC Cycling Club is a group of students who love to ride bikes and explore the outdoors. We organize rides and events throughout the year, and we welcome riders of all skill levels.

Dance

The SMWC Dance Team is a high-energy, coed team delivering entertainment and school spirit. Participants will learn new skills, refine dance techniques, and develop teamwork. It specializes in performing routines in a multitude of genres, including Pom, Jazz, Hip-Hop, and Kick. Interested students must attend tryouts in the spring to be considered for a position on the team. Dancers perform at home football games, home basketball games, and other special events.

Powerlifting

The SMWC Powerlifting Club provides an on-campus community resource for powerlifters and strength athletes looking to train, compete and foster a culture of competition and strength at Saint Mary-of-the-Woods College. Powerlifting allows competitors to compete in three lifts: squat, bench press and deadlift. In competition, members make three attempts in each lift, and the highest attempts are added together to create a combined total. Ranking in a meet is based upon this combined total and the competitor with the highest total is declared the winner in each weight class. In the event of two or more competitors achieving the same total, the competitor with the lightest bodyweight is declared the winner. There are two divisions: equipped or raw. Equipped lifting consists of using a single-ply squat suit, bench shirt and deadlift suit during competition. Knee wraps are also allowed along with wrists wraps. Raw lifting consists of only a singlet and wrist wraps.

Rowing

The SMWC Rowing Club Team is a unique opportunity for students of all skill levels to get active and involved on campus. Regattas (competitions) are held throughout the Midwest and provide a great experience for competing against other collegiate teams. Everyone has a purpose and plays a crucial role in the success of the team. This paves the way for friendships and the development of self-motivation skills along with physical and mental endurance. In the winter months, practices will be held at the Terre Haute YMCA consisting mainly of indoor workouts on an erg (rowing machine). As spring approaches, workouts will be transitioned to rowing on the Wabash River.
