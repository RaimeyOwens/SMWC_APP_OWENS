Huge Dorm Building On Campus

Here you can find events taking place almost every day of the week. Around campus you can see poster with more information or check out the instagram page.

Academic Affairs

At the heart of Saint Mary-of-the-Woods College is the high quality education we bring to every learning experience. Academic Affairs believes student successes begins with a combination of theoretical and applied educational experiences delivered by our faculty that prepares our students for their chosen careers and beyond.

Admissions

No matter where you are in life, the time is now to advance your education. Application to Saint Mary-of-the-Woods College is free and easy. It only takes minutes. Explore academics, meet professors, make new friends. Visit the SMWC campus in Saint Mary-of-the-Woods, Indiana, to experience your own “Avenue Moment.”

Campus Ministry

MWC Campus Ministry is focused on making missionary disciples through opportunities to encounter Jesus in a new and profound way.

Finaincial Aid

Quality, private college education doesn’t have to break the bank. With scholarships and other financial aid to help make college more affordable, you will be well on your way to your goals in no time.

Information Technology

The Information Technology department strives to offer an empowering, technology rich environment and to lead technology initiatives with innovative strategic vision, while delivering exceptional customer service, support, and security to the College.

Registrar

Welcome to the Office of the Registrar. We provide a variety of specialized services to students and faculty including class scheduling, registration, information regarding academic policies, transcripts, records of the academic progress of students and verification of enrollment and degrees.
